## Introduction
Welcome to **Rush Hour: Detective's Challenge**, a text-based adventure game inspired by the iconic "Rush Hour" film series. Players step into the shoes of detectives Jackie Chang and Chris Talker, navigating through challenges and making choices that influence the game's outcome.

## Game Overview
- **Jackie Chang**: Known for methodical investigation and martial arts skills.
- **Chris Talker**: Finds success through luck and unconventional methods.
- **Mission**: Overcome challenges inspired by "Rush Hour" and "Rush Hour 2."

## Features
- **Engaging Stages**: Unique scenarios based on the films.
- **Dynamic Storytelling**: Choices impact the story and character interactions.
- **Character-Driven Gameplay**: Play according to the detective's traits.
- **Replay Value**: Explore different paths and outcomes.

## How to Play
- Respond to prompts by typing your choices.
- Utilize your detective's skills to solve puzzles and interact with characters.
- Combine action, humor, and suspense to navigate the narrative.
